No Images

She does not know
Her beauty
She thinks her brown body
Has no glory.

If she could dance
Naked,
Under palm trees
And see her image in the river
She would know.

But there are no palm trees
On the street,
An dishwater gives back no
images.

William Waring Cuney
Poet of the Harlem Renaissance...
